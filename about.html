Products Description

Sarena is a vertical textiles entity with expertise in all manners of cotton and cotton-blended fabrics and all parts of the value chain.

We service both the fashion wear and institutional work-wear markets from dedicated finishing and manufacturing units. Our fabrics range from 20g p. sqm (~1.45oz) to 450g p sqm (13.23oz) in different weaves and constructions.

Fashion-Wear

We offer an eclectic collection; from yarn dyes in different constructions to digital and rotary printed textures and floral patterns, we offer VAT, Reactive, Aquasol, Pigment and Disperse Dyeing. Some of our super stretch fabrics extend up to 90% with world-class recovery whereas our normal range of power-stretch fabrics can be tailored to our customers’ requirements. Our more premium product lines invariably incorporate the luxurious feel of modal, viscose or Tencel® in some proportion coupled with distinctive textures in both stretch and rigid versions. Please check out our range of topical finishes and some of our best-selling products.

Work-Wear

We offer customized fabrics to meet the most stringent requirements of our customers across a broad spectrum of industry ranging from petro-chemical and oil and gas to automobile, hospitals, hospitality, leasing and so on. We offer specialized products in each of these industries, backed by comprehensive test reports from our fully accredited (ISO17025) in-house laboratory or any third party service provider. Our protective fabrics span a wide variety of functional features. We encourage you to have a look. For a list of our work-wear products, associated accreditations and test reports, please view our product catalogue.
